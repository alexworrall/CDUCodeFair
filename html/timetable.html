CDU Code Fair 2020 is ready for registrations

Registrations are now open for the 2020 CDU Code Fair. The code fair is a place for students to share their IT skills, connect with industry and for students to showcase their creative digital problem solving skills.

Registration

Be at the venue on time for last minute registrations at 8:30am sharp. Please register beforehand if possible.

Register for the event 8:30am

The opening of the 2020 CDU Code Fair will kick off the activities. There will be a Welcome to the country by Larrakia Nation, A speech will then be given by David Young, Dean of College of Engineering, IT and Environment.

Academic excellence awards will be given to IT students who have recieved outstanding marks in their studies. The awards will be presented by Kathleen Robinson
